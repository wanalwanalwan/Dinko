# Privacy Policy

**pkkl AI - Pickleball Skill Tracker**

*Last updated: March 22, 2026*

## Overview

pkkl AI is a pickleball skill tracking and AI coaching app. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

## Account & Authentication

pkkl AI requires an account to use. You can sign up with an email address and password, or use Sign in with Apple. When you create an account, we store your email address and, if provided, your first and last name.

## Data We Collect

### Information You Provide
- **Account information**: Email address, name (optional)
- **Skill data**: Skills you create, ratings, and progress checkers
- **Session notes**: Text you enter when logging practice sessions
- **Drill data**: Drill completions, preferences, and history
- **Onboarding preferences**: DUPR rating range, training frequency, drill type preferences

### Data Generated by the App
- **AI coaching responses**: When you log a session, your notes and skill data are sent to our AI coaching service to generate personalized feedback, skill rating adjustments, and drill recommendations
- **Session logs**: Records of your coaching interactions are stored to improve recommendations over time

## How We Use Your Data

- To provide AI-powered coaching and skill tracking features
- To generate personalized drill recommendations
- To track your progress over time
- To authenticate your account

## Data Storage & Processing

- Your account and session data is stored on secure servers provided by Supabase (cloud infrastructure)
- AI coaching features are powered by Anthropic's Claude AI. Your session notes and skill data are sent to Anthropic's API for processing. Anthropic does not use this data to train their models
- Local data (skill cache, preferences) is stored on your device using CoreData and UserDefaults

## Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. Your data is shared only with:
- **Supabase**: For account authentication and data storage
- **Anthropic**: For AI coaching features (session notes and skill data only)

We do not use analytics or advertising trackers.

## Data Retention & Deletion

You can delete your account at any time from the app (Home > Settings > Delete Account). When you delete your account:
- Your authentication credentials are permanently removed
- Your session logs, drill queue, and roadmap data are permanently deleted from our servers
- Local data on your device is cleared

## Children's Privacy

pkkl AI is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can delete it.

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Access your data through the app
- Delete your account and associated data
- Contact us with questions about your data

## Security

We use industry-standard security measures including encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS), secure token storage (iOS Keychain), and row-level security policies on our database.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.
