# Privacy Policy

**Dinko - Pickleball Skill Tracker**

*Last updated: February 7, 2026*

## Overview

Dinko is a pickleball skill tracking app designed to help players monitor and improve their game. Your privacy is important to us, and this policy explains how we handle your information.

## Data Collection

**Dinko does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers.** All information you enter into the app — including skills, ratings, progress checkers, and session notes — is stored exclusively on your device using local storage.

## What We Don't Do

- We do not collect personal information
- We do not track your location
- We do not use analytics or tracking tools
- We do not use third-party advertising
- We do not share data with third parties
- We do not require an account or login

## Data Storage

All app data is stored locally on your iPhone. If you delete the app, all data is permanently removed from your device.

## Children's Privacy

Dinko does not knowingly collect any personal information from children. Since no data is collected from any user, the app is safe for users of all ages.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.
