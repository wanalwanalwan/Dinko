FAQ

How do I track a skill?

Tap the + button on the home screen to add a skill, then use the slider to rate your current level.

How do I update my rating?

On the home screen, tap any skill to expand the slider. Drag to your new rating and tap Save.

What happens at 100%?

The skill is automatically marked as completed and moves to your Completed Skills count.

Where is my session history?

Go to the Timeline tab to see all your past sessions and skill changes.

Can I delete a skill?

Yes. Open the skill detail page and tap Delete Skill at the bottom.
