## Frequently Asked Questions

**How do I track a skill?**
Tap the + button on the home screen to add a skill, then use the slider to rate your current level. You can also chat with the AI coach to log a full session.

**How do I update my skill rating?**
On the home screen, tap any skill to expand the rating slider. Drag to your new rating and tap Save.

**Can I delete a skill?**
Yes. Tap a skill to open its detail page, then scroll to the bottom and tap Delete Skill.

**What happens when a skill reaches 100%?**
The skill is automatically marked as completed and moves to your Completed Skills count.

**How do I view my session history?**
Go to the Timeline tab to see all your past sessions and skill changes.
